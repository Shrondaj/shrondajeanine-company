About Our Mission

Serving homeless and underserved individuals and families in Phoenix, Arizona

Our Mission

At Shronda Jeanine & Company, we are dedicated to providing compassionate, holistic healing services that nurture mind, body, and spirit. Our community action program focuses on serving homeless and underserved individuals and families in Phoenix, Arizona.

Our Approach

  • Holistic wellness coaching and virtual health consulting
  • Community-centered healing approach
  • Support for addiction and trauma recovery
  • Creating spaces that add value to the community
  • Monthly celebrations and community connections
