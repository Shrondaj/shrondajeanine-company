Welcome to Abundant Harvest

Abundant Harvest is our cottage food program dedicated to creating delicious, homemade baked goods and treats that bring joy and nourishment to our community. Each item is crafted with care using quality ingredients and made with love.

As part of SHRONDA JEANINE & COMPANY, our cottage food program extends our mission of healing and community building by providing employment opportunities and fostering connections through the shared experience of food.
